About Verdant

Your Partner for Industrial Innovation

Established in 2020 as a small innovation hub in Jakarta, Verdant has grown to become one of Indonesia's leading organizations promoting sustainable industrialization. With branches in Jakarta, Surabaya, and Bandung, we're committed to driving technological innovation and infrastructure development aligned with SDG 9.

At Verdant, we believe that sustainable industry and resilient infrastructure are key drivers of economic growth and prosperity. Our team of experts collaborates with businesses, government agencies, and communities to create innovative solutions that balance industrial advancement with environmental sustainability.

Since 2020

Our Mission

Advancing SDG 9 for Sustainable Development

Verdant was founded with a clear mission: to build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization, and foster innovation in Indonesia. We aim to be at the forefront of the nation's transition to a sustainable industrial future.

Our approach integrates economic, social, and environmental considerations into all our projects and initiatives. By working closely with stakeholders across sectors, we're creating innovative solutions that drive economic growth while preserving environmental resources and improving quality of life for communities across Indonesia.
