Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How can my company collaborate with Verdant?

There are several ways to collaborate with us. You can submit a project proposal through our Innovate Now page, become a technology partner, participate in our innovation challenges, or join our sustainable industry network. Contact our partnerships team at partnerships@verdant.id to discuss specific opportunities.

Does Verdant provide funding for sustainable industry projects?

Yes, we have various funding mechanisms for qualifying projects that align with our mission and SDG 9 goals. We offer innovation grants, matching funds, and can connect promising initiatives with our investor network. Each funding opportunity has specific eligibility criteria and application processes.

What types of industrial innovations does Verdant focus on?

We focus on innovations that advance sustainable industrialization and infrastructure development. Key areas include clean manufacturing technologies, renewable energy solutions, sustainable transportation systems, circular economy models, green building technologies, and digital infrastructure that promotes inclusive industrial development.

Can Verdant help with regulatory compliance for industrial innovations?

Yes, our policy experts can provide guidance on navigating regulatory frameworks related to sustainable industrial development. We maintain close relationships with regulatory bodies and can assist with compliance requirements, environmental impact assessments, and industry certifications.

Does Verdant work with international partners?

Absolutely. We actively collaborate with international organizations, research institutions, and companies to bring global expertise and technologies to Indonesia. We also help Indonesian innovations access international markets and funding opportunities. Our international partnerships span across Asia, Europe, and North America.
